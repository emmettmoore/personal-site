Hello! My name is Emmett, and I live in Somerville, MA.

I spent my childhood in Houston, TX, but moved to the greater Boston area just in time for my angsty teenage years.

I graduated from Tufts University in 2015 with a BS in Computer Science. Growing up, both my parents were programmers. It was only natural that I would try to avoid my fate for a semester or two, but I eventually came to follow in their footsteps. I quickly fell in love with the opportunity to use technical skills to solve tangible problems that are important to me.

While in school, I also started rock climbing. I love that climbing requires persistence and creativity, and that it is an independent sport with a great community. I also love that it is best enjoyed in unreasonably beautiful places.

Since graduation, I have applied my interesting in programming to my job as a full stack software engineer at InsightSquared.

When I am not working or climbing, I am usually:

  • Riding my 1980-something Univega around the city until the next thing eventually breaks and I stubbornly try to fix it myself.
  • Working on this site, or various other projects.
  • Listening to new music (I used to play, now I just listen, but maybe someday I'll get back into playing again).
  • Outside.
  • Playing with my (parents') dogs.
  • Reading.
  • Watching the Red Sox.
